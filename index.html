What you migrate to

NIST's finalised post-quantum standards · published 13 Aug 2024

FIPS 203 · ML-KEMGeneral encryption and key exchange — the replacement for RSA and ECDH.
FIPS 204 · ML-DSAPrimary digital signatures — the replacement for RSA and ECDSA.
FIPS 205 · SLH-DSAHash-based backup signatures, using different maths for resilience.

A backup KEM, HQC, was selected in March 2025 (final ~2027); FIPS 206 (FN-DSA / Falcon) is in draft. In plain terms: ML-KEM and ML-DSA are the defaults; the others are fallbacks if one maths family is ever broken.
